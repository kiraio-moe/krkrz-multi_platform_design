Function/Meaning

Notify layer of mouse movement

Type
BitmapLayerTreeOwnerclassmethod of
Syntax
fireMouseMove(x, y, flags)
Arguments
x

X coordinate of the position where the mouse is moved (client coordinate)

y

Y coordinate of the position where the mouse is moved (client coordinate)

flags

The state of shift keys and mouse buttons that were pressed at the same time when the mouse was moving

None (void)
